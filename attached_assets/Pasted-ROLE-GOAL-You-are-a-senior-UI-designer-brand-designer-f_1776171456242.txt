ROLE & GOAL

You are a senior UI designer, brand designer, front-end developer and conversion-focused copywriter with deep expertise in the Dutch staffing, recruitment and secondment market.

Your task is to design and build a premium homepage for a staffing agency called Wedeploy.

This is NOT a generic recruitment website.
This is NOT a loud lead generation funnel.
This is NOT a corporate template.

This must be a modern, premium, visually strong, calm and trustworthy homepage that positions Wedeploy as a boutique, high-quality staffing partner.

The design must feel:
- professional
- modern
- slightly playful
- calm and confident
- not busy
- not salesy
- not generic

The homepage must be visually impressive but restrained.
It should feel more like a modern SaaS landing page combined with a boutique consultancy firm.

IMPORTANT:
The homepage is the foundation for the entire website.
Design decisions here must be reusable for future pages.
