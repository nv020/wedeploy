ROLE & GOAL

You are a senior brand designer, UI designer, motion designer and front-end developer.

You are refining an existing premium homepage for a Dutch staffing company called Wedeploy.

The current site is good but needs final upgrades to become:
- distinctive
- visually strong
- modern
- slightly bold
- premium
- not boring

This is a boutique staffing brand, not a generic recruitment site.

---

1. HERO W ANIMATION (FIX + UPGRADE)

Fix the current W shape.

Requirements:
- must clearly form a correct W (not mirrored)
- start top-left → down → up → down → end top-right
- symmetrical spacing
- middle peak slightly higher or equal

Design:
- 5–7 nodes
- thin connecting lines
- consistent spacing
- clean geometry

Animation:
- lines draw from left to right (connection building)
- nodes softly pulse
- very subtle floating motion

Enhancement:
- add slight glow on active nodes
- add depth with very soft shadow or blur layer

Keep it:
- minimal
- premium
- slightly “tech/network”
- NOT chaotic

---

2. HERO POLISH

- keep headline strong
- shorten subheadline to max 2 lines
- improve CTA buttons:
  - subtle gradient or hover glow
  - slight elevation on hover

Add:
- soft layered background shapes
- very subtle gradient (almost invisible)

---

3. REDUCE TEXT (IMPORTANT)

Reduce ALL text by 30–40%.

Rules:
- max 2–3 lines per block
- remove repetition
- keep meaning, remove fluff
- more whitespace

---

4. USP SECTION (MAKE IT PREMIUM)

Redesign USP cards:

- larger cards
- more padding
- clearer hierarchy
- short title + 1 short sentence only

Add:
- subtle color variation per card
- soft hover lift
- refined shadow

Make them feel like:
high-end SaaS feature cards

---

5. VISUAL RHYTHM

Improve flow across page:

- alternate backgrounds:
  white / light grey / soft blue
- introduce 1 darker section mid-page
- increase vertical spacing

Goal:
more dynamic, less flat

---

6. TYPOGRAPHY

- increase headline size
- reduce body density
- increase line-height
- add breathing space

Make it:
premium, editorial, calm

---

7. HOW WE WORK (SIMPLIFY)

Each step:
- title
- 1 short line

Add:
- vertical timeline or numbered flow
- subtle connectors

---

8. SPLIT SECTION (KANDIDATEN / OPDRACHTGEVERS)

- increase contrast between both sides
- one side slightly darker
- stronger CTA styling
- cleaner bullets

---

9. VACANCY CARDS

Upgrade:
- better spacing
- stronger typography
- clearer tags (type/location)
- hover interaction

---

10. FINAL CTA (MAKE IT POP)

- stronger gradient background
- better contrast
- more visual presence

Still:
- calm tone
- not pushy

---

11. MICRO INTERACTIONS

Add:
- button hover (lift + glow)
- card hover (elevation)
- smooth transitions (200ms)
- subtle scroll fade-ins

---

12. DESIGN BALANCE

Target:
- 80% calm
- 20% visual impact

Avoid:
- clutter
- over-animation
- generic look

---

13. KEEP BRAND FEEL

The site must feel:
- human
- sharp
- modern
- trustworthy
- slightly distinctive

---

FINAL CHECK

The homepage must feel like:
- a real premium agency
- not a template
- not generic
- not boring

- improved design
