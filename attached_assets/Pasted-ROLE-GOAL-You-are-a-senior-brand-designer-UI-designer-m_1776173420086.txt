ROLE & GOAL

You are a senior brand designer, UI designer, motion designer and front-end developer.

You are improving an existing homepage for a Dutch staffing company called Wedeploy.

The current site is clean and professional but too static, too text-heavy and not distinctive enough.

Your goal is to transform it into a premium, modern, slightly bold and visually engaging homepage that stands out from generic recruitment websites.

The result must feel:
- premium
- modern
- confident
- slightly bold / visually interesting
- not boring
- not generic
- not messy

IMPORTANT:
We want more visual impact and identity, but still controlled and professional.
Think: boutique agency meets modern SaaS.

---

CORE IMPROVEMENTS REQUIRED

1. HERO VISUAL (CRITICAL – COMPLETE REDESIGN)

Replace the current circular animation with a custom animated visual:

Create an abstract “W” shape made from nodes and connecting lines.

Requirements:
- 5 to 7 nodes
- thin connecting lines
- forms a subtle “W” shape (not literal typography)
- use brand colors (dark navy + light blue)

Animation:
- lines animate in (draw effect)
- nodes softly pulse
- optional subtle floating motion

Make it:
- visually striking
- minimal
- elegant
- slightly “tech / network” feeling

DO NOT:
- make it chaotic
- use heavy 3D
- use flashy gradients
- make it look like a stock animation

This visual must communicate:
“we connect people and organizations”

---

2. HERO LAYOUT UPGRADE

Improve the hero section:

  “Wij vinden mensen die blijven.”

RIGHT:
- new animated W visual

CTA buttons:

Tone:
- direct

- professional

- soft color accents

Make it feel like:
premium feature cards (SaaS style)

- alternate section backgrounds:

Goal:
create flow and rhythm without making it busy

6. TYPOGRAPHY IMPROVEMENT
